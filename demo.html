All About Sweet Potatoes

The best vegetable there is!

The sweet potato is starchy, large root vegetable with a sweet taste. They are known to contain a relatively high nutritional value, most notably the vitamin beta-carotene. Sweet potatoes have versatile uses for cooking. They can be baked, steamed, fried, microwaved. They are frequently used in savory dishes, curries, soups, burgers, and much more.

Top Producers of Sweet Potatoes

  1. China
  2. Uganda
  3. Nigeria

Fun Facts

  • Sweet Potatoes are only distantly related to potatoes
  • The flesh of sweet potatoes can vary in color to include white, red, pink and purple
  • It is a misconception that orange sweet potatoes are the same as yams
